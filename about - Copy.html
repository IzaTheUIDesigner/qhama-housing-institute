About Qhama

Who We Are

Qhama Social Housing Institute is a registered Social Housing Institution dedicated to providing quality, affordable rental accommodation to low- to moderate-income households in South Africa.

Founded on the principles of dignity, inclusion, and community empowerment, we work to address the critical shortage of affordable housing in urban areas. Our developments are not just buildings—they are homes that foster strong, connected communities.

We believe that everyone deserves access to safe, well-located housing in thriving neighborhoods with access to employment, education, healthcare, and public transport.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and sustainable social housing that empowers individuals and families, strengthens communities, and contributes to inclusive urban development across South Africa.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every person has access to dignified, affordable housing in well-located, thriving communities that offer opportunities for growth, connection, and prosperity.

Our Values

Dignity, Integrity, Community, Sustainability, Excellence, Inclusion. We are committed to treating every resident with respect and delivering housing solutions that stand the test of time.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Qhama Social Housing Institute delivers comprehensive social housing solutions that go beyond bricks and mortar.

Affordable Rental Housing

We provide quality rental units for individuals and families earning between R3,500 and R22,000 per month.

Property Development

We develop new social housing projects in well-located urban areas with access to jobs and amenities.

Community Building

We create safe, inclusive spaces that foster social cohesion and empower residents to thrive.

Property Management

We ensure well-maintained buildings and responsive services for all residents across our developments.
